Balance Seekr is a comprehensive wellness app that helps you build habits, journal your thoughts, complete tasks, and connect with like-minded individuals through Solana-powered Mastermind groups.

Features:
- Daily habit tracking with notes and day-of-week scheduling
- Personal journaling with optional encryption for privacy
- Task management with completion tracking
- Mastermind groups with Solana blockchain payments
- Badge achievement system for motivation
- X (Twitter) account verification and integration
- Customizable background color themes
- Guided breathwork exercises with visual and audio cues

Built with React Native and Expo, featuring full Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter integration for seamless wallet connectivity. The app prioritizes user privacy with optional encryption for sensitive journal entries.

Balance Seekr helps you maintain balance in your life through structured habit formation, mindful reflection, and community connection.
